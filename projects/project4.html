81H, 108 TIMES

Jiaho Ji

I have been spent about a month handwriting ‘Prajnaparamita Sutra’ for 108 times. After finish, I melt the Sutra into the ocean.

Based on Buddhism, I use a kind of meditation practice giving my understanding of born and death, which is the arise and disappear of self- awareness and desire.

Collective desire and consiousness are assembled, exiled, and returned.
