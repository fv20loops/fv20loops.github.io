The past
the present
and the future

Ke Sun

This project intends to combine the mechanically industrial past with the digitally technical future as a way of expressing and transmitting the feeling of transient modernity.

The explosive flow of information in the digital age travels in a cloud space - fragile and unstable.

The wrong flow of information invades every moment like a virus; the super imposed date continually repeating as an accumulation of time.

The petrochemical raw materials leave the trace in the plastic, which has a much longer duration than the items it wraps, the people who bought it or the companies that made it.
